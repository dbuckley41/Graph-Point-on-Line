Is the Point on the Line?

Problem 1: Is the point (2, 7) on the line y = 2x + 3?
Step 1: Plug x = 2 into the equation y = 2x + 3.
y = 2(2) + 3 = 4 + 3 = 7
Step 2: The y-value for x = 2 is 7.
Step 3: The point (2, 7) is on the line.
Problem 2: Is the point (3, 5) on the line y = -x + 8?
Step 1: Plug x = 3 into the equation y = -x + 8.
y = -(3) + 8 = -3 + 8 = 5
Step 2: The y-value for x = 3 is 5.
Step 3: The point (3, 5) is on the line.
Problem 3: Is the point (1, 2) on the line y = 4x - 1?
Step 1: Plug x = 1 into the equation y = 4x - 1.
y = 4(1) - 1 = 4 - 1 = 3
Step 2: The y-value for x = 1 is 3.
Step 3: The point (1, 2) is not on the line.
